## I think like a Bayesian and build like an engineer.

I build systems and tools for workflow automation, data processing, and reproducible engineering environments.

My background spans economics, legal technology, and electronics engineering, where I transitioned from data analysis into system-level engineering and software tooling.

I have worked on:

data pipelines and workflow automation systems in legal and regulatory contexts
ETL and analytics systems built in Python, SQL, and scientific computing stacks
design of reproducible engineering environments (Linux-based workstation, CLI tooling)
structured documentation systems for technical workflows (engineering blog with CI/CD)

My approach to engineering is iterative: I validate ideas by building systems, refining them through feedback, and documenting their evolution.

Currently, I focus on systems-level engineering in EDA and infrastructure-oriented workflows, including reproducible tooling and automation for complex technical environments.
