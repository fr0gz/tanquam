EDA Internship Series #1 - From Tools to Systems

May 2, 2026

Context

During my internship at AC3E /Synopsys, I was exposed to EDA tools and workflows focused on analog layout

Problem

A recurring issue was the friction in the development environment:

  • Dependency on unstable servers
  • Lack of structured tooling
  • Implicit knowledge required to operate tools

Approach

Instead of focusing only on circuit design, i explored how to reduce friction in the sorrounding system:

-reproducible EDA workstation -CLI-based tooling -structured documentation

Takeaways

EDA workflows are not only about tools, but about the systems that enable their effective use.

  • EDA
  • Tooling
  • Infrastructure
